About

A web conference is an online event that facilitates the exchange of information, ideas, and knowledge among participants through a web platform. It allows for remote participation from various locations, eliminating the need for physical presence. Web conferences cover diverse topics and typically include expert speaker presentations, Q&A sessions, and participant interaction through features like chat or forums. The key advantages include easy accessibility, time and cost savings, and flexibility in participation.
